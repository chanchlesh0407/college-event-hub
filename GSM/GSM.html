About Us

We are Team GS Motorsports, the formula student team of SGSITS, Indore. We deal with the designing and manufacturing a formula style single seater open wheeled racing vehicle completely fabricated by a dedicated team of students from various domains who share a common passion towards the automotive sector and are driven by a single goal of a dynamic finish at prestigious formula student events.

Past EVENTS

SUPRA 2022

Supra SAE INDIA, is a national level engineering competition which is organized by SAE India at buddh international circuit, noida, the only F1 track of india. This year the event was of 4 days with first 3 days of static events and last day of endurance

FORMULA BHARAT

Formula Bharat is an Indian Formula Student-style engineering design competition in which students from colleges and universities all over the country, compete with a life-size formula-style vehicle in areas of engineering design, overall cost, marketability and dynamic performance. The series replicates the global student Formula series hosted in around 11 countries per year.

BLITZIT

For the first time in SGSITS, BLITZIT brings an electrifying gaming showdown, uniting gamers from across the campus in an ultimate battle of skill, strategy, and speed. Whether you're a casual player or a hardcore esports enthusiast, this event is designed to push your gaming prowess to the next level. .
