About Us

TThe SGSITS Robotics Club is a hub of passionate innovators, engineers, and tech enthusiasts committed to exploring the fascinating world of robotics and automation. We believe in learning by doing, fostering an environment where students design, build, and program robots for real-world applications. Through hands-on projects, competitions, and collaborative research, we aim to push the boundaries of artificial intelligence, automation, and machine learning to create a better future. To be a leading force in robotics and automation, empowering students to innovate, design, and develop intelligent robotic solutions that revolutionize industries and contribute to technological and societal progress.
