About Us

AICTE has launched a scheme for establishing AICTE-IDEA (Idea Development, Evaluation & Application) Lab in its approved institutions for encouraging students for application of Science, Technologies, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM) fundamentals towards enhanced hands-on experience and learning by doing. As a common facility embedded in the institution, IDEA Lab facilitates the students to “engage, explore, experience, express and excel” as desired by the Hon’ble Prime Minister in the wake of release of National Education Policy 2020. These Labs are mandated to function 24 x 7 in the institutions selected and be available to the colleges, schools and industries around also.

Our MENTORS

Dr. Krishnakant Dhakad

Dr. Krishnakant Dhakar has done his B.E. and M.E. from Jabalpur Engineering College, Jabalpur and Punjab Engineering College, Chandigarh respectively. He obtained his Ph.D. from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Roorkee. He has served as an Assistant Professor at NIT Bhopal and NIT Jalandhar. His research interests include Advanced Machining, Composite Materials, Optimization and Supply Chain Management. He has authored or co-authored more than 35 publications which include journal papers, conference papers, and book chapters. He has also got two patents. He has been PI/Co-PI for five projects and two consultancies. He has been the reviewer of many reputed journals. Further, he has been the guest editor of Materials Today’s. He has presented his research at USA and Japan.

Ms. Priyanka Bamne

Ms. Priyanka Bamne is currently serving as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Engineering. With a strong academic background and years of experience in the field, she has been actively involved in teaching, mentoring, and research. Her primary areas of expertise include the Internet of Things (IoT) and Networking, where she explores cutting-edge technologies, innovative applications, and advancements in connectivity. She is passionate about integrating emerging technologies into real-world solutions and guiding students to develop technical proficiency. Through her research and contributions, she aims to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical implementation in modern computing environments.
