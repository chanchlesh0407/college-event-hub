About Us

At Institute Level NSS unit of the institute has been established on 04-08-2012. Neeraj Kumar Jain has been the Programme Officer of the unit since then. The unit has been working there onwards under the guidance of advisory committee which is chaired by the director of the institute. The unit, since its establishment, has been continuously doing its regular activities with aim of providing the socio-economic exposure of various national problems specially Rural India. The NSS volunteers, besides getting such exposure, also benefitted by certificate recognition through B and C level certificate programmes of the NSS.

VISION AND MISSION

NOT ME

To develop a democratic society with equality by supporting ‘Social Service’ without expectation and keeping sympathy for ‘All Living Being’ by accommodating each other’s angle of opinion

BUT YOU

to foster enthusiasm and high moral values in students especially NSS volunteers through the activities, orientation programmes and camps. To develop students’ personality by Involving them (volunteers) in planning, decision making, organizing and execution of the activities of the unit.

Past EVENTS

NUKKAD NATAK

Nukkad Natak is more than just street theatre—it’s a powerful medium of expression that brings social issues to life. This electrifying event transforms open spaces into stages where performers captivate audiences with hard-hitting narratives, humor, and compelling storytelling. With vibrant performances addressing real-world challenges, Nukkad Natak serves as a voice for change, leaving spectators inspired and awakened. Get ready to witness a theatrical revolution where passion meets purpose!

BLOOD DONATION CAMPS

The Blood Donation Camp is a noble initiative that embodies the spirit of compassion and humanity. Organized to encourage individuals to contribute to a life-saving cause, this event provides a seamless and safe donation experience under expert medical supervision. Every drop counts, and by participating, donors play a crucial role in saving lives and supporting those in need. With a mission to promote awareness and collective responsibility, the camp welcomes everyone to step forward and make a difference—because giving blood means giving life.
