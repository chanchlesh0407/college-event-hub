GS Production House

About Us

GS Production House was established on November 25th, 2018, with the primary goal of recognizing, refining and promoting the talents of students of SGSITS, Indore across various creative spheres. GS Production House is a media production club and the official supervisor of all the social media handles of SGSITS, Indore. We conduct various enthralling events and activities wherein the students can participate and exhibit their skills without any apprehension. We are a student run organisation which provides an intriguing platform for imaginative minds to enhance their creative abilities. GS Production House gives a powerful head start to new learners, transforming them into aspiring and driven individuals.

Our MENTORS

MR. Ashwin Shrivastava

Mr Ashwin Shrivastava, a Professor in the Department of electronics and Telecommunication Engineering and mentor at GSPH, excels in Soft Computing, Machine Learning, and Software Engineering. Since 2007, she has guided numerous projects, published widely, and developed key software for examination management.

Ms. Priyanka Bamne

Ms. Priyanka Bamne is currently serving as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Engineering. With a strong academic background and years of experience in the field, she has been actively involved in teaching, mentoring, and research. Her primary areas of expertise include the Internet of Things (IoT) and Networking, where she explores cutting-edge technologies, innovative applications, and advancements in connectivity. She is passionate about integrating emerging technologies into real-world solutions and guiding students to develop technical proficiency. Through her research and contributions, she aims to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical implementation in modern computing environments.

Past EVENTS

Media Summit

The Media Summit is a convergence of storytellers, content creators, and industry experts discussing the ever-evolving landscape of digital media. This event features insightful panel discussions, interactive workshops, and expert-led sessions on the latest trends in video production, post-production, and media technology. Attendees will gain valuable insights into storytelling techniques, cinematography, and the impact of AI in media. Whether you’re a filmmaker, a digital creator, or a tech enthusiast, the Media Summit is your gateway to the future of content creation.

Khoj

Khoj is a thrilling exploration into the world of storytelling, creativity, and visual magic. This event invites aspiring filmmakers, editors, and visual artists to push their creative limits and craft compelling narratives. From short film screenings to on-the-spot filmmaking challenges, Khoj is a celebration of innovation and imagination in video production. Whether you’re a beginner experimenting with a camera or an experienced filmmaker looking for fresh inspiration, this event is the perfect platform to showcase your talent and bring your vision to life.
