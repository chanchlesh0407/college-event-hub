Computer Club

About Us

The club aims to groom students technically and personality wise and help them achieve their potential, make them ready for the bright career they desire. It is organised by the students for the students. Various Activities Conducted by Club are: Code with the Flow: An online game designing competitionThe participants were able to design the games like snake and ladder and its checks using Visual Programming language Scratch Developed by the Lifelong Kindergarten group at the MIT media lab. Workshop on Web Development: The participants were taught useful IDEs, HTML, CSS, Javascript and given introduction of JSP and Servlet and introduced to Sublime, Netbeans, Echilips, jvm, jdk. Workshop on Latex: The participants were taught IDEs for tex document development and syntax to build their presentations, resume, Reports etc. using various online and offline IDE’s Overleaf, texEditor, texMaker etc.

Roboacalypse Event

Robocalaypse event was just the end of a phase of Learning robotics and IOT, with an assurance of many more to follow. The classroom was filled with positive learning environment on both the days incorporating participation from both, mentors as well as students, through an action-based approach. Hope you had just as much fun attending!

Progate Week of Learning

It was a hands-on session of the skills that the participants had learnt over the week as a part of Week of Learning. We coded-along to create a complete web application, most participants made one for the first time. It was an inexplicable feeling to be able to help so many people explore new technology and to help them upskill for their future...
